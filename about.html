Competition Rules

Dates and Format

The first round of FBA will be completely online. Registration will be open from September 21st, 2017 to January 29th, 2018. Participants are required to submit the following:

  • a one-page research briefing with clear indication of research direction and sources of evidence.
  • a two-to-three-minute video of participants talking about the importance of their research topic, addressing the risks and opportunities it can bring to future humanity.

Twenty finalists will be announced on Sunday, February 11th. The finalists will have until March to prepare the following for the final round which will be held at a date to be determined at the beginning of March::

  • a two-to- three-page formal research paper with deep analysis on the issue. Citations of evidence is required.
  • a three-to- four-minute speech similar to the video of the first round. PowerPoint aid is allowed.

The final round will take place in a local auditorium or gymnasium. Research papers are to be submitted the previous night. The participants will be giving their speech on the stage, and the judges will score them. Speech score will not be immediately announced, and a final score will be calculated with speech score and research paper score each weight fifty percent. After a lunch break, the final score will be announced and awards being given to the winners.
