Sumbission Guideline

To be considered as a valid entry, all you need is to submit a 1-2 minute video and 1-2 paragraph summary that addresses three parts:

First, identify the issue: “Is it a risk or an opportunity for space development?”

Second, reference a few past or current publications addressing the issue. These will provide you with the issue’s background and further your thinking.

Lastly, propose a creative and imaginative solution to the issue. If your solution is reasonable and supported by some form of research, it works!

Don’t worry if some researchers have already published articles on your idea. As long as you clearly explain your idea and persuade us that your solution is the BEST solution among all submissions, you are well-prepared to win a prize.
