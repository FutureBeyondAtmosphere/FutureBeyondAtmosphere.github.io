Vice Director General

My name is Eva Smerekanych and I am in the Newport High School class of 2019. I have been deeply fascinated by space ever since I was really little, and have enjoyed pursuing it in various ways from simply looking up at the night sky, to becoming the Vice President of Newport's Astronomy club, to driving 19 hours round trip to Oregon for last August's total solar eclipse.
