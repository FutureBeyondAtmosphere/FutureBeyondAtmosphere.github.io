About Us

About Us

Humanity was never this close to the full-on exploration of the dark abyss of space; it will take place in our century. However, both risks and opportunities will accompany us as we dive deeper. Future Beyond the Atmosphere is a competition that encourage current high school students to explore such risks and opportunities. We are students from across four high schools in the Greater Seattle region, and we all share the passion for the space and the imagination for the future. We hope through this competition we can generate innovative thoughts and discussion, bring awareness to the importance of the space, and in the process inspire one another.
